### Market structure differences
- Kalshi contracts are CFTC-regulated, settle in USD. Polymarket is crypto-based (USDC).
- Kalshi has thinner liquidity on Iran markets (~$100K) vs Polymarket ($1M+). Wider spreads on Kalshi.
- Kalshi has stricter resolution criteria (e.g., 7-day MA >60 for Hormuz) vs Polymarket's more subjective definitions.
- No clean arbitrage found yet — markets structure contracts differently. Will continue monitoring for identical-event price divergences.
